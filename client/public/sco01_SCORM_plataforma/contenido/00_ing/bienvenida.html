Now that you know the main elements of the JEDI Path, it's time to make some stops!

With each stop, you will learn details about Inclusive Action and Inclusive Leadership.

In the final part of the JEDI Path journey, you will encounter different situations where you must act and make a decision based on what you have learned. For each resolved situation, you will earn a postal stamp that you can collect in your FEMSA World Passport.
