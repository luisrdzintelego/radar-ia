Immersion to JEDI

Welcome to the start of your JEDI Path tour at FEMSA!

Let's explore the essential elements of justice, equity, diversity, and inclusion that are core to our team. The JEDI path is developed at FEMSA. Everything we do has a clear purpose; to improve people's lives. We transform everyday life into well-being and growth by always being available to offer solutions and experiences that turn every day into something extraordinary.

Besides impacting the lives of those around us, we are also commited to the social and economic development of the communities where we are present, our employees and our company, maintaining our commitment to caring for our planet. To strenghten our purpose, we rely on the FEMSA Principles; our compass to guide our behaviors and actions, that enables us to grow and adapt to changes in the world without losing our essence.

Since 1890, FEMSA has been guided by a visionary and humanistic spirit. In 2018, the vision was laid out around diversity, equity, and inclusion, reflecting our commitment to building a more just, equitable, and sustainable future for all. We aspire to be a mirror of our consumers, acting with closeness and respect. JEDI is an enabler for our businesses, empowering us to move towards more distant and inspiring horizons.

JEDI Vision

To become the most sustainable, diverse, equitable and inclusive organization, leading the way in the communities where we operate.

Based on the above, we created the JEDI Model, which is based on 4 principles:

JUSTICE

EQUITY

DIVERSITY

INCLUSION

The objective of the JEDI Model is to promote a safe, inclusive work environment that promotes the growth of all people. We want these pillars to guide our daily actions, our daily tasks, and our relationships with colleagues, strengthening an environment of respect and collaboration, therefore:

JUSTICE

To have a fair company is to design the path.

Play the video for more information.
Show transcript

Do you know what justice is?

FEMSA knows that having a fair company is much more than a goal. Guaranteeing an environment where everyone has the same opportunities is a responsibility.

Justice should be the cornerstone of our processes and policies. It means designing or adjusting systems that are fair for all, no matter their circumstances, and making sure every person has the same opportunities to access, grow, and develop, without giving up who they are.

Justice is like designing a road. We must ensure it is evenly paved and with clear signposts and enough light, so that it is accessible, and everyone can travel on it safely.

This is why we have pledged to a transparent governance and management system, where accountability is essential.

Growing together means breaking down barriers that impede our own or other people’s development and, apart from enhancing opportunities, be ourselves and contribute all our talent, be authentic and have a sense of belonging.

Join us on the path to justice! Together, we can make a difference.

EQUITY

Being equitable means ensuring that all people can reach the same destination, no matter where we start from.

Do you promote equity in your environment?

At FEMSA, this means the approaches and resources adapt to the needs and contexts of each person.

In our road, equity can compare to access ramps, crossings, bike lanes, signposts, or other facilitators that let everybody advance on the path according to what they need.

In other words, equity becomes a tool that adjusts to the environment and systems, so they adapt to the individual needs of each person.

We have made a commitment to identifying tactical gaps and to reviewing and adjusting key processes, such as recruitment, hiring, and accessibility so we can guarantee the same conditions for everyone.

Together, we can build an environment where every person feels valued and respected. Thank you for joining us in our commitment to equity!

Do you know how we experience diversity at FEMSA? Fostering diversity means treasuring and celebrating the richness of each individual.

We see diversity as all the different channels where people can travel on the JEDI road, and how it contributes something unique to the journey and enriches the experience on this shared road.
Some people walk, others ride bikes or cars, and some skate.

Diversity means opening spaces, including, and boosting other points of view to ensure access and development in our company, regardless of diversity.

We are committed to embracing the JEDI model, which guarantees the conditions that make people feel valued, included, and respected for their singularities, differences, and skills.

Join us on our way to achieving diversity at FEMSA! Together, we make a more powerful and more diverse team, where every individual feels welcomed and can contribute their value and shine.

INCLUSION

Fostering inclusion in our company is to make sure that every type of person feels welcome and safe along the way.

How do you experience inclusion? Fostering inclusion means creating safe and trusting environments where everybody feels welcomed, valued, and free to be who they are.

Inclusion means making sure every traveler feels welcomed and safe on the road. For example, making sure there are enough resting areas, pedestrian crossings, special lanes for bicycles, safety elements for the visually impaired, and even charging stations for electric vehicles.

At FEMSA, we create an environment where every person feels welcomed and valued, by identifying and managing our own prejudices and by being aware of our biases, we make sure everybody has the chance to grow and shine.

We are committed to adjusting our systems to guarantee access and growth to each diversity through an inclusive organizational culture that includes activities for education and for raising awareness, an internal and external communication strategy, and the training of JEDI ambassadors.

Join us in this path to inclusion at FEMSA! Together, we can build a culture where every person feels a sense of belonging by being accepted for who they are and by being valued and acknowledged.

By applying the JEDI model, not only do we empower an engaged and diverse team to reach its full potential, but we also foster collaboration. This, in turn, expands our capabilities and growth both individually and organizationally.

How does the JEDI Model contribute to FEMSA?

Sustainability of our businesses.
Development of human capital.
Social and economic progress of the communities in which we operate.

Apply justice, equity, diversity and inclusion in your daily life and remember that this is not a destination, but a path that allows us to learn and move towards our shared vision.

No matter how we travel, the important thing is that we move forward together, because together we are FEMSA.
